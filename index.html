My name is Arman Cohan and I am a PhD student in computer science at Georgetown University . Currently, I am affiliated with IR lab, part of the computer science department and I am working with Prof. Nazli Goharian. My main areas of interest include NLP, Machine Learning, IR and their applications in health informatics.

About

Before joining Georgetown I obtained my masters degree from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic) in E-Business in 2013. My undergraduate years were spent in the same school, where I obtained my Bachelors degree in Information Technology in 2010.
